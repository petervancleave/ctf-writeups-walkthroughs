By completing this task, you will:

• Understand the basics of Defense in Depth  
• Learn why layered security is critical  
• Identify practical implementations of Defense in Depth

---

Contrasting the Past and the Modern Takes on Defensive Security

Traditional security relied heavily on perimeter defenses such as castle walls. Once breached, the defenders were often finished. Modern security avoids single points of failure by protecting multiple layers including credentials, processes, data, and human behavior.

---

Disrupting Adversarial Objectives

Defense in Depth disrupts attackers at multiple stages. Even if one layer fails, additional layers increase detection, delay progress, and prevent full compromise.
